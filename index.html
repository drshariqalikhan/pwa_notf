Help Me Quit!

Resources & Support

Embarking on a journey to quit a habit is a commendable step. Here are some strategies:

  • Understand Your Triggers: Identify situations or emotions that lead to the habit.
  • Set a Quit Date: Choose a specific date to commit to your goal.
  • Develop Coping Mechanisms: Find healthy alternatives for when cravings strike (e.g., exercise, hobbies).
  • Build a Support System: Share your goals with friends, family, or support groups.
  • Track Your Progress: Celebrate milestones to stay motivated.
